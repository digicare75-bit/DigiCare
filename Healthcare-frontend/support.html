Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Log in to your patient dashboard, search for a doctor in your preferred specialty, and click on "Book Appointment". You can then select a convenient time slot.
Are the doctors verified?
Yes, every doctor on HealthCare+ undergoes a rigorous verification process, including background checks and medical license verification, to ensure the highest quality of care.
How does the online pharmacy work?
You can browse medicines by category or search for specific ones. Once you place an order, it is verified and shipped from our licensed pharmacy partners directly to your address.
Is my health data secure?
We use industry-standard end-to-end encryption and comply with healthcare data privacy regulations to ensure your personal and medical information is always protected.
